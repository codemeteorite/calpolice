# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Any form of violence or threats

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
